About Internet of Things

What is IoT?

IoT means physical devices connected with internet or network so they can send data, receive commands and work automatically.

Simple Meaning of IoT

Internet of Things connects real-world devices like smart bulbs, AC controllers, smart fans, door sensors, cameras, smart locks, speakers and appliances with a mobile app or voice assistant.

Example: When you say “Hey Google, turn on Study Mode”, your room light, fan, AC and music can automatically adjust according to your preference.

  • ✅ Connected devices
  • ✅ Mobile app control
  • ✅ Voice assistant support
  • ✅ Automation routines
  • ✅ Security alerts
